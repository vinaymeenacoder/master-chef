## Ingredients
***

### for dough:
***

* 2 cup wheat flour
* ½ tsp salt
* water (for kneading)
* 2 tbsp oil
 
 
 ### for aloo stuffing:
 ***

* 3 potato / aloo (boiled & mashed)
* 1 tsp ginger paste
* 2 chilli (finely chopped)
* 2 tbsp coriander (finely chopped)
* ½ tsp coriander seeds
* ¼ tsp ajwain / carom seeds
* 1 tsp chilli powder
* ½ tsp cumin powder
* ½ tsp garam masala
* ¾ tsp aamchur
* ½ tsp salt


### other ingredients:
***

* wheat flour (for dusting)
* oil (for roasting)
